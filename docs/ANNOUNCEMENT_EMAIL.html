What is ComplianceOS?

ComplianceOS helps you manage your vendor compliance lifecycle in one place:

  • Vendor tracking — Onboard, review, and manage vendors with automated compliance checklists
  • Document management — Track contracts, W-9s, insurance certificates, and security questionnaires with expiry alerts
  • Compliance frameworks — Built-in support for CJIS, FedRAMP, StateRAMP, and CISA CPG with control-level tracking
  • Risk assessment — Evaluate vendor risk with structured questionnaires tied to tier levels
  • Audit trail — Every action is logged with timestamp, actor, and entity for compliance-ready reporting
  • Reports & exports — Pre-built reports, CSV exports, and print-ready PDF compliance reports

Why ComplianceOS?

  • Encrypted by default — All data encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM (the same standard required by CJIS and FedRAMP). We practice what we preach.
  • Zero backend required — Runs entirely in your browser. Your data stays on your machine (or your Google Drive).
  • No vendor lock-in — Export everything to CSV at any time.
  • Built for small teams — Not a stripped-down enterprise tool. Built from the ground up for businesses with 3-50 vendors.
  • Privacy-first — No tracking, no analytics, no data collection. Your compliance data is yours.
  • Rigorous testing — 72 automated tests covering encryption, business logic, permissions, and framework definitions. Full CI/CD pipeline with lint, type-check, test, and build on every commit.
